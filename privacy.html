Yume Privacy Policy

Effective: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how realroz collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Yume Discord application.

1. Information We Collect

Yume may collect and store:

  • Discord user IDs.
  • Discord server, channel, and message IDs when needed.
  • Usernames and display names for interface and support purposes.
  • Commands, button interactions, and timestamps required to operate Yume.
  • Gameplay data, including cards, inventory, balances, crystals, titles, wishlists, statistics, trades, marriages, Realm progress, cooldowns, and rewards.
  • Administrative and security logs involving important account or gameplay changes.
  • Purchase identifiers, product identifiers, entitlement records, transaction status, and virtual items granted.
  • Information you voluntarily provide in support messages or bug reports.

Yume does not receive or store your full payment-card number. Payment information is processed by Discord or the payment provider identified during checkout.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Operate Yume’s commands and gameplay systems.
  • Save and display your progress.
  • Process and fulfill virtual-item purchases.
  • Prevent duplicate rewards, fraud, automation, and abuse.
  • Investigate bugs, disputes, and security incidents.
  • Provide support and enforce Yume’s rules.
  • Maintain backups and service reliability.
  • Comply with legal and platform requirements.

3. How We Share Information

Information may be shared with:

  • Discord, as necessary to operate the application and use Discord’s services.
  • Hosting, database, monitoring, and payment providers that help operate Yume.
  • Authorities or affected parties when reasonably required by law, legal process, platform policy, safety, or fraud prevention.

Yume does not sell personal information to advertisers.

4. Data Retention

Active gameplay data is generally retained while needed to operate Yume and maintain user progress.

Database backups may be retained for up to 30 days. Security and administrative audit logs may be retained for up to 90 days, unless a longer period is reasonably necessary for fraud, disputes, security, legal compliance, or service recovery.

Some transaction, enforcement, and backup records may remain after a deletion request when retention is reasonably necessary for legal compliance, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, or system integrity.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect Yume’s data. However, no online service or storage system can guarantee absolute security.

Yume staff will never ask for your Discord password or Discord authentication token.

6. Data Access and Deletion Requests

You may request information about your stored Yume data or request deletion of eligible data by contacting:

realrozbiz@gmail.com

Include your Discord user ID. Additional verification may be required to confirm that the request concerns your account.

Deleting Yume data may permanently remove cards, inventory, balances, statistics, titles, Realm progress, marriage progress, and other saved features.

7. Children and Eligibility

Yume is intended only for people who are permitted to use Discord under Discord’s applicable terms and local law.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Yume changes. The effective date will be revised when material updates are published.

9. Contact

Operator: realroz
Privacy and support email: realrozbiz@gmail.com
